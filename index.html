Research

Currently, my research focuses on 3D scene understanding and scene interaction. My subsequent research is aimed at embodied scene interaction, exploring efficient scene representation strategies. My goal is to enable robots to autonomously perceive spatial environments so that they can execute various downstream tasks based on human instructions.
